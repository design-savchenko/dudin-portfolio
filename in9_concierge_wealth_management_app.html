In9 - Concierge wealth management app (Soon)

UI/UX | Application | Graphic design

The value of this app is that it helps to save time and make decisions quickly. What to wear, for what event, in what weather or season, taking into account local traditions and preferences, as well as based on the user's wardrobe. It is a platform for stylists who can not only expand their client base but also share their experience. You can find a personalized specialist according to your preferences and get a wide range of fashion services.

Task

Design an MVP and branding of a simple and minimalistic application that will help the user quickly choose what to wear, taking into account the surrounding circumstances, weather, and seasons, national and geographical preferences of the population, based on the wardrobe created by the user. In addition, to create a working platform for fashion professionals (stylists) to find clients and provide their services.

Solution

A simple, minimalistic cross-platform application with a limited set of basic functions. Simple and intuitive user interface and minimalist design, taking into account modern behavioral patterns, and with a moderate color palette.
